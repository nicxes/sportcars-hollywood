EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO KNOW
  • How Sport Cars Referral Program Work?

    The Sport Cars Referral Program is a program that rewards customers who refer new customers to the dealership. If the person referred buys a pre-owned vehicle for under $15,000, the referrer will receive $300, and for purchases over $15,000, the referrer will receive $500.

  • How can I join to the Referral Program?

    To join the Referral Program, simply fill out the referral form on the dealership's website. Once you fill out the form, you will be eligible to receive a referral fee if your referral purchases a car.

  • Is there a limit to the number of referrals I can make?

    No, there is no limit to the number of referrals you can make. The more people you refer to PA Auto Sales, the more money you can make.

  • How do I receive my referral fee?

    If your referral purchases a vehicle from Sport Cars Miami and cites you as the referrer, the dealership will send you a check for the referral fee up to $500.

  • Are there any conditions for my referrals to be valid?

    Yes, there are conditions for your referrals to be valid. Your referral must fill out the referral form before they visit Sport Cars to buy a car, and they must be a new customer. The referral form must also be submitted before the purchase or a voucher must be presented at the time of the referral's purchase. Sport Cars will not honor a voucher/form that has been submitted after the purchase has been completed.

  • Where can I get more information about the Referral Program?

    For more information about the Referral Program, you can call the dealership at 833-617-5627 and talk to their customer support representative.
